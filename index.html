About D&D Dentistry


D & D Dental Clinic is located in the heart of Ahmedabad, just 300 meters off SG highway in Chanakyapuri. We aim to provide quality dental care using hi-tech equipment in a clean, sterile and peaceful environment. Our clinic is equipped with the latest instruments and the best materials. Using AERB certified digital radiographs and intraoral cameras, we can show you about your dental condition. Our focus is to not only treat your dental condition, but to educate you about your oral hygiene so as to prevent any oral infections.
